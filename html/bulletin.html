Welcome to the Bulletin Board!

Here you can learn more information about upcoming meetings and events. All students are welcome to attend, including those who are not mixed asian. Generally, we meet weekly, every thursday of the academic year, alternating between general body meetings (GBMs) and fundays. Meetings are held at 8pm in Greenlaw, room 222 unless otherwise noted. Come hang out with us, and make sure to bring your friends!
